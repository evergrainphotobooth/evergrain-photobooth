Our Story

A long time coming.

We've been hosting for years — long before Evergrain had a name.

Themed parties. Backyard birthdays that turned into productions. Costume nights because we asked, and our people always said yes.

What we love isn't really the event itself — it's what happens inside it. Conversations picking up mid-sentence, like no time has passed. Photos from those nights aren't just pictures. They're proof. The grain of something you'll come back to.

Evergrain Photobooth is everything we love about hosting, finally built into a service we get to bring to your event. We sweat the details — the booth, the backdrop, the lighting, the small touches that say somebody thought about this — so you can be fully there with your people.

A night worth keeping. Etched in the grain.

With love,
Cindy + Hamilton
