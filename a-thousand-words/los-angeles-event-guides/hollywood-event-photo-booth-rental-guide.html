Avoiding Common Photo Booth Pitfalls

Even with the best intentions, a few missteps can diminish the photo booth experience.

  • Poor Lighting: Professional studio lighting is crucial for flattering results.
  • Generic Props: Avoid cheap, flimsy props that don't match your event's theme or luxury aesthetic.
  • Lack of Attendant: A dedicated attendant ensures smooth operation and guest assistance; a photo booth rental with attendant is crucial.
  • Unclear Pricing: Always get a detailed quote outlining all costs, including setup, breakdown, travel, and add-ons.
  • Low-Quality Prints: If offering prints, ensure they are high-resolution on quality paper, reflecting your event's standard.
