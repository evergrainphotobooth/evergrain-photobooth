What we make

A photobooth that earns its place at every event.

We started Evergrain because most photobooths felt like an afterthought — fluorescent lights, grainy prints, forgettable backdrops. Ours is built around a real DSLR, intentional lighting and a setup that looks like part of the design plan, not against it.
